Refunds & Cancellation Policy

Riget Zoo Adventures Ltd
Address: Stafford College, Victoria Square, Stafford, ST16 2QR
Contact: sophie@riget-zoo.ltd

1. Introduction

This Refunds & Cancellation Policy explains how refunds, cancellations, and amendments are handled for tickets, hotel bookings, and any other paid services offered by Riget Zoo Adventures Ltd. By making a purchase, you agree to the terms outlined below.

2. Ticket Refunds & Cancellations
2.1 Standard Day Tickets

Tickets may be cancelled for a full refund if requested at least 48 hours before the scheduled visit date.

Cancellations requested within 48 hours of the visit date are not eligible for a refund.

Missed visits (no-shows) are non-refundable.

2.2 Event or Special Attraction Tickets

Refunds for special events must be requested at least 7 days before the event date.

Within 7 days, tickets are non-refundable unless the event is cancelled by Riget Zoo Adventures Ltd.

2.3 Weather Policy

The zoo operates in all typical weather conditions.

Refunds will not be provided due to poor weather unless the zoo itself is forced to close.

3. Hotel Booking Refunds & Cancellations
3.1 Standard Refund Terms

Cancellations made 7 days or more before the check-in date are eligible for a full refund.

Cancellations made between 48 hours and 7 days before check-in receive a 50% refund.

Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable.

3.2 Early Departure

Guests who leave earlier than planned will not receive a partial refund for unused nights.

3.3 No-shows

Failure to check in without prior notice will result in the booking being marked as a no-show and no refund will be issued.

4. Amendments
4.1 Ticket Amendments

You may request a change of visit date once, free of charge, if submitted at least 48 hours before the original visit.

Additional changes may incur an administrative fee.

4.2 Hotel Booking Amendments

Date amendments are allowed up to 7 days before the stay, subject to room availability.

Changes within 7 days may incur additional charges based on room rates and availability.

5. Zoo-Initiated Cancellations

If an event, ticketed visit, or hotel booking is cancelled by Riget Zoo Adventures Ltd due to emergencies, staffing issues, safety reasons, or operational problems:

You will be offered either:

A full refund, or

A free rebooking to another available date.

6. How to Request a Refund or Cancellation

To make a refund or cancellation request, please contact:

Email: sophie@riget-zoo.ltd

Please include:

Your full name

The email address used for booking

Booking reference number

The service (ticket or hotel stay)

Reason for cancellation (optional)

We aim to respond within 5 working days.

7. Payment Processing

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method originally used.
Processing times depend on your bank or payment provider and may take up to 10 working days.

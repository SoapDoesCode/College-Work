Last updated: 05/12/2025

Riget Zoo Adventures Ltd ("RZA", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled safely and responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

We operate the Riget Zoo Adventures website, which provides attraction information, educational material, ticket booking, hotel reservations, and account management features.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services.

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you create an account or make a booking, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (securely stored and hashed)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party payment providers; we do not store full card details)
  • Booking details (e.g., visit dates, number of guests, hotel preferences)

2.2 Cookies

We use essential cookies only.

These are used for:

  • Keeping users logged into their account
  • Maintaining session information

We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process ticket and hotel bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and important service emails
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the functionality and security of our digital services
  • Maintain website access (login sessions)

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

4. Payment Processing

When purchasing zoo tickets or booking a hotel stay, payments are handled securely by approved third-party payment processors.

These processors may collect and store your payment information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

RZA does not store full credit/debit card numbers.

5. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data (UK GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contractual necessity - to complete bookings and manage your account
  • Legitimate interests - improving site security and functionality
  • Consent - for the use of essential cookies where required
  • Legal obligations - record-keeping for financial reporting

6. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to keep your data safe. This includes:

  • Storing passwords using secure hashing
  • Restricting access to personal data
  • Using HTTPS to protect data in transit
  • Secure data storage practices

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Account information: kept while your account remains active
  • Booking and payment records: retained for legal and financial requirements
  • Cookies: expire automatically after their session or validity period

You may request account deletion at any time (see Section 9).

8. Sharing Your Information

We may share limited data with:

  • Payment processors (for handling transactions)
  • Service providers that help operate the website (e.g., hosting)

We never share your data for marketing or advertising purposes.

9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Receive a copy of your data (data portability)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

Email: sophie@riget-zoo.ltd

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for unsupervised use by children under 13.

Bookings and account registration must be completed by a responsible adult.

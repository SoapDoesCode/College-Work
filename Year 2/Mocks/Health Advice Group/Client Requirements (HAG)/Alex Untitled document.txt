How does legislation and regulations affect the project and development of the software
Legislation and regulations affect the project by requiring that personal data like location health information and login details be collected stored and processed securely with user consent and Accessibility laws mean the software must be usable by people with disabilities such as supporting screen readers and keyboard navigation and Health advice provided by the app may be subject to medical or public health regulations so incorrect advice could create legal liability and Location tracking requires consent and secure handling of geolocation data and Security standards demand secure coding encryption and regular updates and Consumer protection laws require accurate information about the apps capabilities to avoid misleading users and Overall these regulations influence the design development and maintenance of the software and ignoring them can lead to legal financial and reputational risks

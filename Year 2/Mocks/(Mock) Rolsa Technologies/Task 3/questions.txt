Technical:
* What is your name?
* What is your age?
* Is the layout intuitive and easy to navigate?
-> if no:
  * What was wrong with the layout? Why was it not easy to navigate? How would you improve it?
* How easy was it to find the navigation bar? Do you think the average user would find it easy to use? What are your thoughts on the logo?
* How would you rate the CSS?
  * Maintainability?
  * Readability?
  * Efficiency?
  * Reusability?
* Did input validation work as expected?
-> if no:
  * Why did input validation not work as expected? What page/form was it on? What data did you use? What happened?

Non-technical:
* What is your name?
* What is your age?
* Is the layout intuitive and easy to navigate?
-> if no:
  * What was wrong with the layout? Why was it not easy to navigate?
* How easy was it to find the navigation bar? Was it easy to use? What are your thoughts on the logo?
* Does the website look modern and professional?
-> if no:
* What would you say makes it not modern? Why is it unprofessional?

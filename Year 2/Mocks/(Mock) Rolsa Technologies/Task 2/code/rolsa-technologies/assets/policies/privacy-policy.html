Privacy Policy - Rolsa Technologies

Last updated: 05/12/2025

Rolsa Technologies ("Rolsa", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled safely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

1. Who We Are

Rolsa Technologies Address: Stafford College, Victoria Square, Stafford, ST16 2QR Email: sophie@rolsa.tech

We operate the Rolsa Technologies website and digital platform, which provides information about green energy products and services, including solar panel installation and maintenance, electric vehicle (EV) charging stations, and smart home energy management systems. Our platform also allows users to book consultations and installations, calculate their carbon footprint, and track energy usage.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services.

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you create an account, book a consultation, or use our tools, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (securely stored and hashed)
  • Consultation or installation details
  • Information entered into tools such as the carbon footprint calculator or energy usage tracker
  • Payment information (if applicable), processed securely via third-party payment providers; we do not store full card details

2.2 Cookies

We use essential cookies only. These are used for:

  • Keeping users logged into their account
  • Maintaining secure session information
  • Supporting core platform features

We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Schedule and manage consultations and installations
  • Provide access to tools such as the carbon footprint calculator and energy tracking features
  • Send confirmations and important service-related emails
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the functionality, accessibility, and security of our digital services
  • Maintain secure login sessions

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

4. Payment Processing

Where payments are required (for example, deposits or services), transactions are handled securely by approved third-party payment processors.

These processors may collect and store your payment information in accordance with their own privacy policies. Rolsa Technologies does not store full credit or debit card numbers.

5. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data (UK GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contractual necessity - to manage consultations, installations, and account services
  • Legitimate interests - improving platform security, accessibility, and functionality
  • Consent - where required for the use of essential cookies
  • Legal obligations - record-keeping for financial and regulatory purposes

6. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to keep your data safe. This includes:

  • Storing passwords using secure hashing
  • Restricting access to personal data
  • Using HTTPS to protect data in transit
  • Applying secure data storage practices

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Account information: kept while your account remains active
  • Consultation and service records: retained for operational and legal requirements
  • Financial records: retained for legal and tax purposes
  • Cookies: expire automatically after their session or validity period

You may request account deletion at any time (see Section 9).

8. Sharing Your Information

We may share limited data with:

  • Payment processors (for handling transactions)
  • Service providers that help operate the website (e.g., hosting providers)

We never share your data for marketing or advertising purposes.

9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Receive a copy of your data (data portability)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: sophie@rolsa.tech

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and digital services are not intended for unsupervised use by children under 13. Account registration and consultation bookings must be completed by a responsible adult.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top will always show when it was last updated.

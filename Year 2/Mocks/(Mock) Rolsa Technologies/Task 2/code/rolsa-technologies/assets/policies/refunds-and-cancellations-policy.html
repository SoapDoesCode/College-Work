Refunds & Cancellation Policy - Rolsa Technologies

Rolsa Technologies Address: Stafford College, Victoria Square, Stafford, ST16 2QR Contact: sophie@rolsa.tech

1. Introduction

This Refunds & Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations, refunds, and amendments are handled for consultations, installations, and any paid services offered by Rolsa Technologies. By booking a service or making a payment, you agree to the terms outlined below.

2. Consultations

2.1 Free Initial Consultations

If your consultation is free of charge, you may cancel or reschedule at any time. We ask that you provide at least 24 hours' notice where possible.

2.2 Paid Consultations

If a consultation requires a fee or deposit:

  • Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled appointment are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of the appointment may be non-refundable.
  • Failure to attend without notice (no-show) is non-refundable.

3. Installation Services (Solar Panels, EV Charging Stations, Smart Energy Systems)

3.1 Deposits

Where a deposit is required to secure installation:

  • Deposits are fully refundable if cancellation is requested before materials are ordered or work is scheduled.
  • Once materials have been ordered or preparation work has begun, part or all of the deposit may be retained to cover incurred costs.

3.2 Cancellations Before Installation Date

  • Cancellations made at least 7 days before the agreed installation date may be eligible for a full or partial refund, depending on costs already incurred.
  • Cancellations made within 7 days of the installation date may result in administrative or restocking fees being deducted from the refund.

3.3 After Installation Has Started

Once installation work has begun, refunds will not normally be available for completed work or materials used. Any concerns about workmanship or service quality should be raised promptly so they can be addressed.

4. Amendments and Rescheduling

4.1 Consultation Appointments

Consultations may be rescheduled once free of charge if requested at least 48 hours before the original appointment time. Additional changes may incur an administrative fee.

4.2 Installation Dates

Installation dates may be amended subject to availability. Changes requested at least 7 days in advance are typically free of charge. Changes within 7 days may incur additional costs depending on scheduling, labour, or material arrangements.

5. Rolsa-Initiated Cancellations

If Rolsa Technologies needs to cancel or reschedule a consultation or installation due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., safety concerns, supply issues, weather conditions affecting safe installation, or operational reasons), you will be offered either:

  • A full refund of any payments made, or
  • A rescheduled appointment at no additional cost.

6. Digital Tools and Online Services

Access to online tools such as the carbon footprint calculator and energy usage tracker is provided as part of our digital platform. As these tools are informational and provided digitally, they are not eligible for refunds.

7. How to Request a Refund or Cancellation

To request a cancellation, amendment, or refund, please contact:

Email: sophie@rolsa.tech

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address used for booking
  • Booking reference (if applicable)
  • The service booked (consultation or installation)
  • Reason for cancellation (optional)

We aim to respond within 5 working days.

8. Payment Processing

Refunds will be processed using the original payment method wherever possible. Processing times depend on your bank or payment provider and may take up to 10 working days.
